ABOUT US

At Future Flims Theater, we are dedicated to providing our customers with the best movie-going experience possible. Established in 2014 at Junction Square in Kamaryut Township, Yangon, we have been entertaining our audiences with the latest blockbusters and timeless classics for years. Our state-of-the-art theaters feature the latest audio and visual technology to deliver an immersive and unforgettable movie experience. From the comfortable seating to the delicious snacks, we make sure that every aspect of your visit to our theater is enjoyable and memorable.
